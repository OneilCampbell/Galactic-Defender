History


  • The year was 2097.

  • We had perfected Space travel and discovered portals that can transport us through time and space.


  • Unfortunately, our expeditions were not to go unnoticed.

  • Soon enough we began to cross paths with intelligent alien life forms.


  • And with their intelligence came Agendas and the desire to acquire all the resources that our planet has to offer.

  • Inevitably, they began to attack, one after the next, hell-bent on conquering our world.


  • At first, we held them off, holding our own in this galactic warfare.

  • We showed the more malevolent aliens in the galaxy that we were not to be trifled with.


  • However when it became evident that these aliens were unrelenting in their pursuit for galactic conquest, it was decided that we needed to establish a task force to keep these creatures at bay.

  • Enter the Rangers:


  • Originally tasked with InterGalactic Defense

  • The Rangers soon became protectors of the planet from all global threats, both foreign and domestic


  • Now they travel the galaxy keeping tabs on potential threats and eliminating active ones

  • So the next time you are out star gazing on a cool Summer's Eve, or simply wishing for warmth upon a shooting star on a wintry night, just remember

  • The Rangers will always be there to keep you safe
