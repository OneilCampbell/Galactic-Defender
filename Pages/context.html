Mission Brief

Past Galactic Enemies


  • The rangers have been around for Decades, and as such, they have defeates many a foe in their time

  • Arguably, the most formidable of these foes has been Praenuntiae Mortis:

  • The Harbringers of Death


  • After a month long battle across 5 galaxies and 2 time periods via the time portals, Praenuntiae Mortis was defeated and disbanded.

  • All of their surviving members were captured and imprisoned.

  • All, execpt one.

  • Galactimus: Destroyer Of Worlds


  • The brains behind all of Praenuntiae Mortis' operations, Galactimus managed to escape during the aftermath of the battle

  • His affinity for technology, and his ability to use his helmet to manipulate it, meant that he was able to hijack an air craft and flee


  • Fast Forward 5 years later, and Galactimus has assembled a new team and returned.

  • They caught the Rangers by surprise and ambushed them. They took them prisoner in attempt to force them to reveal the location of his former crew members.

  • No one knows where he has taken the rangers, or even where to begin looking for them. All we have is this brief transmission from Red Ranger's helmet, before it lost power
